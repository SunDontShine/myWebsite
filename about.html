Hello, My name is Carlos.

I was born and raised in Chandler, Arizona. At a young age my family decided to move to a small town. I graduated from high school in 2006. A year after graduation I decided to join the Marine Corps. I deployed to Helmand Province, Afghanistan from 2010-2011 at the age of 22.
I love the Marine Corps and my first intention was to make it a career. However, graduating from a University was a personal goal of mine. Prior to receiving orders to exit the Marine Corps in March, 2013 I was enrolled and accepted into the
Arizona State University's Software Engineering program I attended my first classes in the fall semester 2013. Now, I will graduate in the spring semester 2017. My primary focus areas are Web and Mobile applications, as well as Embedded systems programming
