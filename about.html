About me

I was originally born and raised in Chandler, Arizona. At a young age my family decided to move to a small town outside of the city. I graduated from high school in 2006. Young, and eager to create new experiences, I decided to become the first in my family to join the United States Marine Corps. It was filled with fullfilling and life-changing experiences. I deployed to Helmand Province, Afghanistan from 2010-2011 at the age of 22. After my return, I continued my college education at a local college in San Diego.

I love the Marine Corps, and it was first intention to make it a career. However, another personal goal of mine was become the first in my family to graduate from a University. Prior to receiving orders to exiting the Marine Corps in March, 2013 I enrolled and was accepted into the

Arizona State University's Software Engineering program. I attended my first classes in the fall semester 2013. Now, here I am, graduating in 2017 spring semester. My primary focus areas are Web and Mobile applications, as well as Embedded systems programming
